About us and our services

Emiglo Enterprises established in the year 2005 is situated off Kangundo road in Nairobi.It is a company that deals with Health and Safety equipments;we focus on ensuring our clients' needs are met successfully and on time.

Our offering includes PPE(personal protective equipments/safety wear)i.e:

  • hand gloves
  • masks
  • overalls
  • aprons
  • dustcoats
  • headgear
  • respiratory products
  • safety shoes
amongst others.

Emiglo Enterprises has a dedicated team that ensures that strict deadlines and the highest quality products are provided for our clients.

Other Services

Other than equipping our clients with protective wear,we also provide Printing/branding services.

We assist our clients brand their protective wear when requested to.We also provide Digital Printing,Commercial Printing,Laser Engraving & Laser Cutting services.

We also deal with domestic and industrial detergents.

Vision

To provide Kenya and even East-African countries with quality and innovative Personal Protection Equipment and Supporting Services,for creating safer workplaces.

Mission

To be leader in Productive and Supply of PPE and a link between new technologies in safety and industries within East African Region and beyond.

Values
